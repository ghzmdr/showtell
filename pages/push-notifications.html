Push Notifications

PN are great way to trigger user engagement
When used properly they can be a great way add value to the UX by providing content in the right moment and context.
When used improperly they became a reason for the user to uninstall your intrusive app and lower it's trust in your brand.

A notification can be consdered properly crafted if:

  • Timely - Is triggered in the exact moment it's needed.
  • Precise - Shows the minimum amount of informations needed
  • Relevant - Adds information on the user's context, prompts for quick reactions

PNs are implemented trough Service Workers, which (after being installed) subscribe themselves to the server.
This way, whenever a notification is sent, the SW will be woken up to handle that message.

Propel - client-side library, Web Push - NodeJS library
